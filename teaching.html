Teaching Background

My teaching career began as a surf instructor on Vancouver Island in 2009 (Pacific Rim National Park, Ucluelet). From there I continued to teach music lessons as well as English as a second language to adults and children in Canada, various volunteer projects in Latin America, and in the public education system of Spain (Auxiliares de conversación program).

If interested in music lessons contact me for more information!
